About Me

I'm Daniel Angelo C. Nadal. An aspiring artist and illustrator who wants to focus on frontend and UI/UX design as a medium for my works. Inspired by the greatest minds of the rising generation, I would like to tackle a world of aesthetic and comprehensive designs for the future technologies. In the future of my professional career, I want to innovate new designs and techniques for a comprehensive and aesthetic navigation. I would like to focus and work on my skills on startup companies and build a network that allows me to market my skills to the masses.

Logical

Whether it's a story plot, a puzzle, or even a conversation, I naturally break things down and try to see the bigger picture through the small details. I guess you could say logic is my way of navigating the world—it keeps things grounded. I enjoy asking questions, following trails of thought, and solving problems in a way that makes sense, not just feels right.

Creativity

Creativity is kind of my default setting. Whether I'm sketching out ideas, designing a layout, or just daydreaming, my mind's always spinning with visuals and "what ifs." I love turning abstract thoughts into something tangible—something people can feel or interact with.
